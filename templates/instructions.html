Instructions

  1. Click 'Record' on the left sidebar.
  2. You need to record yourself reading the paragraph provided. Have a quick look.
  3. Click the microphone button when you are ready. The microphone will record your voice for 23 seconds. A countdown timer will be shown.
  4. Once the 23 seconds is up, you will be directed automatically to a page showing the guess of your origin country made by the application.
  5. You will be asked if the guess is correct.
    • If the guess is correct, choose the tick and you will be shown the basic information of your country.
    • If the guess is incorrect, choose the cross and then choose your origin country. You will be then shown the basic information of your country.
