Accent Identifier

Have you ever wondered where people around you are from?

This application can identify a person's English accent and guess which country they are from.
Then, this application displays basic information about the country, restaurants around you serving that country's cuisine and the number of students from that country studying in UCL.

Please select the options on the left sidebar to navigate around this application.
Click 'Instructions' on the left sidebar to know how to use this application.
